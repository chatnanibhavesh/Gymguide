How it all got started?

Gymguide is created by Mr. Bhavesh Chatnani, a fitness enthusiast who thought fitness and gym related information should be available to everyone,regardless of other things. He felt like people were more focussed on monetarey gains or appearnace than they were on good health. With a lot of hardwork, good intention and dedication, Gymgyuide was created. This site absolutely doesnt support the use of anabolic suppliments. Bhavesh's aim is to provide people with relaible fitness information and continue to promote fitness as a necessity these days.
